# Supporting Documents

- `plan/workstreams.md` – detailed workstream objectives, deliverables, and dependencies derived from individual reverse brief files.
- `plan/backlog.md` – reverse-brief-by-reverse-brief backlog translating requirements into concrete execution tasks.
- `plan/priorities.md` – scoring matrix (Value/Effort/Difficulty/Urgency) that sequences initiatives while documenting dependencies.
- Future additions: sprint backlogs, RAID logs, testing strategy, and change-management plans as execution progresses.
