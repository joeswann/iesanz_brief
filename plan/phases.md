# Phase Structure

- **Phase 0 – Discovery & Architecture**: confirm requirements, finalise tech stack, map data models/integrations, and validate budget/risks.
- **Phase 1 – Core Platform Build**: implement CMS, membership engine, payments, and baseline integrations (Stripe↔Xero, CMS↔CRM).
- **Phase 2 – Experience & Programs**: deliver awards, education, events, store, forms automation, and analytics.
- **Phase 3 – Optimisation & BOT Transition**: harden security, performance, automation, and provide training, documentation, and handover artefacts.
