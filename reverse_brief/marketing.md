# Marketing Reverse Brief

- **Strategic Objectives (Purple Giraffe Action Plan)**
  - Increase awareness of IESANZ among lighting professionals, students, academics, and corporate partners.
  - Drive membership growth/retention, deepen engagement via value-driven comms, and position IESANZ as leading authority in AU/NZ lighting.
  - Align campaigns with organisational objectives: advocacy, education uptake, awards participation, sponsorship revenue, ESG leadership.

- **Audience & Positioning**
  - Target segments: engineers, architects, designers, manufacturers, students/emerging professionals, academics, corporate affiliates, government stakeholders.
  - Address SWOT: leverage heritage, accredited education, advocacy strength; mitigate outdated digital perception, inconsistent branding, under-engagement of younger professionals.
  - Expand reach to underrepresented groups and APAC markets while maintaining core AU/NZ focus.

- **Messaging Pillars & Value Proposition**
  - Promote access to education, networking, certifications, and industry recognition; spotlight sustainability/ESG leadership and policy influence.
  - Use storytelling (member success, case studies, interviews, white papers) to counter weak content utilisation noted in marketing audit.
  - Emphasize transparent membership tiers, competitive pricing, exclusive resources, and collaborative community.

- **Channels & Content Mix**
  - Owned: website (headless frontend), blog/news, knowledgebase, download portal, email newsletters (networking, bulletins), PR newsroom.
  - Shared/social: LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, X/Twitter, YouTube/podcasts, webinar platforms; embed tagged feeds on site.
  - Events & community: webinars, virtual conferences, local meetups, mentorship programs, branded merchandise, media spokesperson initiatives.
  - Paid/sponsored: structured partner programs, sponsorship packages tied to awards/events/education, chapter-level advertising with national guidelines.

- **Planning & Execution**
  - Develop chapter-specific marketing guidelines/templates plus centralized content library of brand-approved assets (Purple Giraffe “Key strategies”).
  - Maintain marketing & communications calendar covering awards cycles, education launches, conference promos, membership renewals, advocacy pushes.
  - Deliver on rebrand tasks from Next Steps: refreshed logo, new copy/imagery, updated print materials, and enforcement of consistent branding across all platforms.
  - Coordinate with Reverse Brief board portfolios to ensure messaging consistency across conferences, awards, education, marketing, memberships.

- **Automation & Tooling**
  - Integrate CMS with Mailchimp/Campaign Monitor, Buffer, CRM, and analytics for audience sync, automated journeys, and segmentation (grade, chapter, interests).
  - Track campaign KPIs: member growth/retention, social reach/engagement, web traffic, email opens/CTR/unsubscribes, sponsorship pipeline.
  - Apply UTM standards, connect to analytics/datalake dashboards, and surface insights for admin/super-admin reporting.

- **Governance, ESG & Compliance**
  - Enforce approval workflows to prevent off-brand chapter messaging; provide training aligned with BOT model.
  - Ensure Spam Act/GDPR compliance, consent/suppression handling, and accessibility/inclusion in content.
  - Highlight ESG commitments: sustainable lighting advocacy, diversity/inclusion, governance transparency; weave into campaigns per marketing plan.
